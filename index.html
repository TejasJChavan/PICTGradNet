Why PICTGradNET?

Free to Use

At PICTGradNet, we believe in accessibility. That's why our platform is completely free to use for all PICT students. We understand the financial constraints students may face, and we're committed to providing a valuable resource without adding to their financial burden. With PICTGradNet, you can access all the essential placements data and features without any hidden costs or subscription fees.

Reliable Data

Trust is paramount when it comes to making important career decisions. That's why PICTGradNet sources its data directly from officially published sources, ensuring accuracy and reliability. You can have peace of mind knowing that the information you find on our platform is up-to-date and verified, giving you the confidence to make informed choices about your future.

Increase Productivity

Time is precious, especially during the hectic job search process. PICTGradNet aims to streamline your experience and boost your productivity by providing already formatted data. Say goodbye to tedious manual data entry and formatting tasks. With PICTGradNet, you can focus your time and energy on what truly matters – preparing for interviews, networking with professionals, and landing your dream job.
