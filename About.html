PICTGradNet
PICTGradNet is an exclusive platform designed for the vibrant community of Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT). Dedicated to fostering seamless interaction between students and prospective employers, PICTGradNet serves as the go-to destination for both talent acquisition and career advancement opportunities.
For Students: At PICTGradNet, students gain access to a comprehensive directory of companies actively recruiting within the college premises. From internship opportunities to full-time positions, students can explore a myriad of career options tailored to their skills and aspirations. Additionally, PICTGradNet empowers students to showcase their talents through detailed resumes, enabling them to stand out in the competitive job market.
For Companies: For companies seeking top-tier talent, PICTGradNet provides a streamlined platform to connect with the best and brightest minds from PICT. Employers can browse through student profiles, review resumes, and engage with potential candidates directly. With PICTGradNet, recruiting the perfect fit for your organization has never been easier. Join PICTGradNet today and unlock a world of opportunities for students and companies alike!
